LocalChatTrans Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-04-20

This Privacy Policy explains how LocalChatTrans ("we", "our", "the app") handles user data. We are committed to data minimization and local-first processing.

1. Data We Process

  • On-screen chat text (local processing): used to provide translation in supported chat apps.
  • Network state: used to detect connectivity and manage offline model downloads.
  • Subscription status: used to enable paid features via Google Play Billing.

We do not sell personal data. Chat text is not uploaded to our server for advertising purposes.

2. Permissions and Purpose

  • BIND_ACCESSIBILITY_SERVICE: read visible UI text to provide translation features requested by the user.
  • SYSTEM_ALERT_WINDOW: display translation overlay above supported chat apps.
  • INTERNET: download/update offline translation models and handle billing-related communication.
  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE: determine network availability (Wi-Fi/mobile).
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS: show service status notifications (Android 13+).
  • FOREGROUND_SERVICE: keep translation service running reliably.

Users can revoke permissions at any time in device settings.

3. Data Sharing

We do not share chat content with third parties for marketing or profiling. Subscription payments are processed by Google Play and governed by Google's policies.

4. Data Retention and Security

We retain only data necessary to provide app functionality. We apply reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect data.

5. Children's Privacy

The app is not directed to children under 13. If you believe a child provided personal data, contact us and we will review and delete it where applicable.

6. User Choices

  • Disable permissions in Android settings.
  • Uninstall the app at any time.
  • Manage or cancel subscriptions in Google Play.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version will be posted on this page with the updated effective date.
