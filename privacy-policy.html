LocalChatTrans 隐私政策

生效日期 / Last Updated: 2026-04-20

欢迎使用 LocalChatTrans。我们重视您的隐私与数据安全。本政策说明我们如何收集、使用、存储和保护信息。

Welcome to LocalChatTrans. We value your privacy and data security. This policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect information.

1. 我们处理的信息 / Information We Process

1.1 聊天文本（本地处理）

应用通过无障碍服务读取您在受支持聊天应用界面中可见的文本，用于本地翻译和在浮窗中展示结果。该聊天内容默认不会上传到开发者服务器。

The app uses Accessibility Service to read visible text in supported chat apps for local translation and overlay display. Chat content is not uploaded to the developer's server by default.

1.2 设备与网络状态信息

为下载/更新离线翻译模型、判断网络可用性（Wi-Fi/移动网络）以及保障基础功能，应用会使用网络状态相关信息。

To download/update offline translation models, detect network availability (Wi-Fi/mobile), and keep basic features working, the app processes network-state related information.

1.3 订阅相关信息

如您购买订阅，支付与订单由 Google Play Billing 处理。我们仅接收与订阅状态相关的必要结果信息，不会获取您的完整支付卡信息。

If you purchase a subscription, payment and order processing are handled by Google Play Billing. We only receive necessary subscription status results and do not receive your full card details.

2. 权限说明 / Permissions

  • BIND_ACCESSIBILITY_SERVICE: 读取界面可见文本以进行本地翻译（需您手动授权）。
  • SYSTEM_ALERT_WINDOW: 在聊天界面上显示翻译浮窗。
  • INTERNET: 下载/更新离线模型、处理订阅相关通信。
  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE: 检测网络状态以优化下载行为。
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS: 显示服务状态通知（Android 13+）。
  • FOREGROUND_SERVICE: 在前台服务模式下稳定运行翻译功能。

您可随时在系统设置中撤销相关权限。撤销后，相关功能可能无法使用。

You can revoke these permissions at any time in system settings. Related features may stop working after revocation.

3. 数据共享 / Data Sharing

我们不会将您的聊天内容出售给第三方，也不会将聊天文本用于广告定向。订阅支付由 Google Play 处理，受 Google 的隐私政策约束。

We do not sell your chat content to third parties, nor use chat text for ad targeting. Subscription payments are processed by Google Play and subject to Google's privacy policy.

4. 数据存储与安全 / Data Storage and Security

应用尽量采用本地处理策略。我们采取合理安全措施防止未经授权访问、披露或篡改。

The app follows a local-first processing approach whenever possible. We take reasonable safeguards to prevent unauthorized access, disclosure, or tampering.

5. 未成年人隐私 / Children's Privacy

本应用不面向 13 岁以下儿童提供服务。若您认为我们可能收集了未成年人信息，请联系我们，我们将尽快处理。

This app is not intended for children under 13. If you believe we may have collected a minor's information, please contact us and we will handle it promptly.

6. 您的权利 / Your Rights

  • 您可通过系统设置关闭权限或卸载应用。
  • 您可通过 Google Play 管理或取消订阅。
  • 您可联系我们咨询隐私相关问题。
  • You can disable permissions in system settings or uninstall the app.
  • You can manage or cancel subscriptions through Google Play.
  • You can contact us for privacy-related requests.

7. 政策更新 / Policy Updates

我们可能根据法律法规或产品变化更新本政策。更新后将通过本页面发布，并更新“生效日期”。

We may update this policy due to legal requirements or product changes. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
